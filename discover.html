Oswego High School

Oswego High School is one of two high schools located in Oswego, IL and it is our oldest. Built back in 1964 this high school has served as one of the main high schools that students can attend. Oswego High School has 2 Middle Schools that feed into it, Thompson and Traughber

Oswego is not a small town with a population of 35,316 per our most recent census. This means that our high schools are quite large. Oswego High School currently has an enrollment of 2,890 students from Freshman - Seniors for the 2022-2023 school year. This means there are plenty of opportunities to join clubs or sports with such a large and diverse student population. Townships that can go into Oswego High School are Oswego, Montgomery, Aurora, and Joliet

Kendall 11 Movie Theater

Kendall 11 is one of the more popular attractions that Oswego has to offer. It features 11 auditoriums with 2 GDX theaters to help enhance the movie viewers experience. This is easily one of Oswego's favorite attractions among our resident's

Kendall 11 also offer's quite a lot to further enhance your experience. They offer free refills on every size popcorn, soda, and even ICEE's. Before 6 pm, all tickets start at $7 for the normal theaters and $10 for the GDX theaters. After 6 pm, prices go up to $9 with children age 2-11 and Seniors 60 and over at $7. The GDX theater prices stay at $10 for children age 2-11 and Seniors 60 and over, but go up to $12.50 for general admissions.

On Tuesday's they even run a special where all-day, tickets are only $5. If popcorn and snacks didn't fill you up and your are looking for an after movie treat, there is even an Oberweis Ice Cream right across the parking lot. Come check out Kendall 11 for yourself and experience all it has to offer!

Springbrook Village/Farmington Lakes

This is really a great area in Oswego, IL if you are looking to move here, whether you are looking to rent or to buy. This quite little area/village sits between Douglas Rd and Fifth St in Oswego, IL. It is right around a mile behind one of Oswego's main attractions, our Kendall 11 movie theater!

This area really is a quiet and family friendly area. When driving down Fifth St, you get a beautiful view of Farmington Lake that has amazing sunsets during the summer. It even has a path that runs around the entire village and lake. This path is great wether you are looking to go for a run, go on a walk with your, or even ride your bike.

This area is split into two parts. The first is the Springbrook Village area which has townhomes, with varying floorpans. This is a great spot if you are looking to buy your first home or you are looking for something small. The second area if the Farmington Lakes Apartments, which offer luxurious and up to date apartments. There are four 1 bedroom and 1 bathroom floorplans, and two 2 bedroom and 2 bathroom floorplans to choose from. They offer great amenities, such as a newly renovated clubhouse, a 24/7 fitness center, a community pool with a sundeck loung and outdoor grilling station, and a dog park.

Hudson Crossing Park

Hudson Crossing Park is Oswego's most popular public park, located in downtown Oswego and right off of the Fox River. There are multiple areas in which you can explore that can suite your needs.

This park sits on 6.2 acres of land, giving you plenty of space for activities. There is a reservable picnic shelter for private events. There are open picnic areas that even have grills to use. There are even designated fishing areas, with canoe access to the Fox River. They even offer a playground for children to play on and trails on the Fox Rover Trail.

With having such a large space, you can find events that peak your interest. Some of their more popular events hosted here are Brew at the Bridge which features breweries from all over Illinois along with food vendors and live music. It's most popular yearly event is Wine on the Fox which is typically help the first weekend of May. It features wines from Oswego's own Fox Valley Winery and craft beers from Oswego's own Oswego Brewery. There are plenty of food concessions to choose from and plenty of entertainment with all kinds of live musical performances.
